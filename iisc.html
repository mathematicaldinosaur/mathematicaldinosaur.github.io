IISc CSA M.Tech research interview experience

Hi, I’m Sreesankar. On May 13th 2025, I appeared for the research interview at IISc computer science department. In this post you can nd my detailed interview experience and some preparation methods I used for my interview.

Background: After GATE results, IISc called for applications to research programmes. This includes two programmes : M.Tech research and Phd. Selection procedure happened together for both of them. On 16th April, I got the interview call letter, where they mentioned my interview date ( May 13 ). Few days after that, the CS department circulated a google form - where we need to select a research sub stream, two research sub areas, and two background subjects. A table attached below for reference:

Please note that selection of substream is very important because you cannot change it once you are admitted to the programme. But selection of sub area and background subject is only for the purpose of interview. Since I am a theory lover, I selected Theoretical computer science. I selected Combinatorics and Algorithms as my sub areas, Discrete math, Probability as background subjects.
